If you are looking at this page because you are having trouble with Mondo then please go to Support for details of how to receive technical support.

Mondo is a collaborative effort by dozens of contributors and thousands of users (a.k.a. beta-testers *wink*). If you are interested in taking part then please contact us.

If Mondo does not work for you then please bear in mind the following:

  • It works for most computers, most Linux distributions, most users and most hardware/software combinations

  • Your problem might be unique; to find out, ask on the mailing list to see if anyone else has experienced something similar
  • The bug might actually be in another piece of software - for example, your pthreads library or your tape streamer's kernel-level driver
  • It is hard for us to troubleshoot a problem if we do not have access to your exact hardware and software configuration
  • If you would like us to make Mondo run perfectly on your exotic tape streamer then please contribute financially or, even better, offer to loan us said exotic tape streamer

If you would like to contribute financially in return for 1-to-1 technical support, please click on the PayPal button at the top of the page (or e-mail me for my mailng address if you would prefer to write a check).
